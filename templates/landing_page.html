Our Intent


The Vaccination Record Tracking Website is a user-friendly platform designed to store children's details and recommend appropriate vaccines. It includes a general page on the importance of timely immunization. Utilizing three databases, it stores child records, vaccine details, and lists hospitals providing vaccinations. Inspired by the need for clear and effective immunization strategies, the project aims to ensure children are fully immune by age 18 amidst evolving diseaseÂ variants.
