Our Promise


At VaxPulse, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users' personal information. We collect only essential data necessary for providing vaccination-related services and improving user experience. This may include personally identifiable information such as name, contact details, and demographic data. We utilize industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration of user information. We do not sell, trade, or rent users' personal information to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Information may be shared with trusted third-party service providers solely for the purpose of delivering our services effectively. Users have the right to access, modify, or delete their personal data upon request. By using our website, users consent to the collection and use of their information as outlined in this Privacy Policy. Any changes to this policy will be communicated through our website.
